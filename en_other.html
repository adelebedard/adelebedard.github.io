Musical ball

In the context of the course "Tangible media and physical computing", I created a ball that plays music when thrown. My goal was to create a playful object, that would incite to play differently.

To do this, I used Arduino, with an IMU, which measures acceleration, magnetism and angular speed, with an amplifier and speakers inside the ball.

Website : AMPR

Click here to see the website.

In the context of a summer workship, I worked at the creation of a website for the Association des mentors et partenaires de rétablissement, which is a non-profit organisation that employs people suffering from problematics of mental health, to help them reintegrate the working sphere. Presided by Jean-François Pelletier, searcher at Université de Montréal, the organisation does, among other things, research on recovery in mental health. Since I did not know the organisation beforehand, I had, to write the content of the website, to question the employees to understand their vision of the organisation, to be able to make a website that would resemble them. I also participated to the creation of short videos on recovery in mental health. I mostly did the editing, since I had a certain experience, but I also learned texts to deliver them in front of the camera.

Secondaire en spectacle

Before doing Cégeps en spectacle, I participated two times to Secondaire en spectacle. The first year, I composed a song, voice and guitar. The second year, I choreographed a dance, with two friends. We won the first place of the creation category at the local final, which allowed us to present our choreography to the regional final.
