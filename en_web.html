Here is my final project in my web creation course. I decided to do a project aiming to make users realize the omnipresence of child labour in our everyday life. For that, my website asks the user to compose their usual breakfast, by dragging and dropping aliments in the plate. Then, they can see the proportion of children who work for their breakfase, where do these children come from and the number of children that may be involved in their breakfast's production. They can after modify their breakfast as they want, and see these values change in reel-time.
