Performances

Le vent l'emportera

The contest « Cegep en spectacle » allows Cegep students to produce themselves on a stage, doing any form of art that they want. The only restrictions are that it has to be less than 10 minutes and in French. That formula really inspired me, because I could do anything I wanted. Each year, I chose a theme and explored how I could deliver the message or story I wanted to tell, including practically every form of art. The first year, I explored the theme of timidity, because it is something that I have been struggling with my whole life. I wanted to say that I did not have to express myself only with words, but I could do it with art and non-verbal communication. I chose to use juggling balls to represent the words, so I juggle with them, trying to be confident with them, but it does not work very well, so I throw them away and start dancing, feeling more free.

La tête haute

The second year, I decided to tell the story of someone who has an incurable disease and to represent the emotions that she feels when she learns that dhe has only a couple weeks left to live. I chose a song by the « Cowboys Fringants », la tete haute, to be the conductive thread of the piece. I mixed singing, with guitar, and dancing to create a whole piece. The dancing part represents the mix of emotions happening in the protagonist’s mind when she learns the news. The shadow that is appearing at a certain point represents how the character is struggling against her own body, but can’t fight it ; she just has to accept it. I created the video of the shadow using only a white sheet in a doorframe, enlightened with my living room lamp, and I changed the coloration while editing it.
